Honoring Sudhin Dasgupta

The Maestro of Melodies

Sudhin Dasgupta (1912-1968) was a legendary composer who defined the soundscape of Bengali cinema's golden age. His compositions like "Ei Poth Jodi Na Shesh Hoy" and "Phire Aye Chole Aye" continue to resonate across generations.
